About

Who We Are:

The Sunway Zero Hunger Initiative is a passionate and dedicated movement spearheaded by Sunway Group, a global leader in community building and sustainable development. Our mission is to combat hunger and malnutrition by fostering awareness, encouraging community action, and supporting sustainable solutions that ensure everyone has access to adequate, nutritious food.


Our Mission:

  • We believe in the fundamental right of every individual to have access to adequate, nutritious food. Our initiatives are driven by empathy and a deep concern for those affected by hunger and malnutrition.
  • We understand that addressing hunger requires a collective effort. We work closely with various stakeholders, including non-profits, businesses, government agencies, and community members, to create a unified front against hunger.
  • We are committed to promoting sustainable practices in agriculture and food production to ensure that our efforts have a lasting impact on food security and environmental health.

Our Team:

  • Local leaders who understand the unique challenges of their communities and work tirelessly to address them.
  • Professionals who provide valuable insights into improving dietary practices and nutritional outcomes.
  • Dedicated individuals who contribute their time and effort to make a tangible difference in the fight against hunger.
  • Experts in innovative farming techniques that promote sustainability and increase food production.

Our Partners:

  • Partners such as Feeding America and The Trussell Trust who support our food distribution and community outreach efforts.
  • Universities and schools that help us spread awareness and engage the next generation in our mission.
  • Businesses that provide financial support, resources, and volunteer participation to strengthen our initiatives.
  • Local and national government bodies that assist in policy advocacy and program implementation.

Join Us

We invite you to join the Sunway Zero Hunger Initiative in our mission to nourish the future. Together, we can create a world where no one has to suffer from hunger. Whether through volunteering, donating, or simply spreading the word, your involvement can make a significant impact.
